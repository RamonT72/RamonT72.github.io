Animated Landscape

Tool(s): Adobe Animate, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Firefly

This animation was made using Adobe Animate. I sourced my images from Adobe Firefly, I chose a pirate theme, I picked out pirate ship and added birds and a dolphin. Then I imported them to the canvas and I used motion tweens to create the animation. The ship goes from right to left and also has a reflection in the water. I also made the dolphin to a spin as it comes out of the water.
