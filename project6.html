Book Cover

Tool(s): Adobe Illustartor, Chat Gpt, Adobe Firefly

This image was AI genrated using the text to image feature on Adobe Firefly. First I used Chat Gpt to come up with different possible book titles about pirates and adventures. After I picked a title that I liked, I went into Adobe Firefly and used the text to image tool, I typed in words that could relate with the title like:pirate ship, ocean, gold, etc... There were many options to choose from but this one caught my eye. After that I went onto Illustrator to add the title and subtitle to the image.
