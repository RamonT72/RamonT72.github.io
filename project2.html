Movie Poster

Tool(s): Adobe Ilustrator

This is a movie poster I created for the book, The Nine Billion Names of God. I used Adobe Illustrator for this poster. I made a space like background that looks like a gravitational force with a blue to black gradient. I also added 9 stars, referencing the title, they have a gradient from light to dark to give it a glowing effect.
